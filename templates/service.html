What types of psychological services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive psychological services including professional consultancy, counselling/psychotherapy, and training programs. Our services cover individual, couple, family, and group therapy sessions, as well as organizational mental health programs.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book appointments through our website, by calling us directly at 0758283613, or by emailing mwasawellservices@gmail.com. We offer in-person, telephone, and online sessions for your convenience.

What are your service fees and payment options?

Our services are affordably priced with consultation fees starting from KSh 3,500. We accept M-Pesa payments and other mobile money transfers. Detailed pricing is available on our services page.

Are your services confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of our practice. All sessions and client information are kept strictly confidential in accordance with professional psychological ethics and Kenyan data protection laws.

Do you offer services for organizations and companies?

Absolutely. We provide organizational mental health services including employee assistance programs, mental health awareness training, leadership coaching, and workplace psychological safety assessments.

What qualifications do your psychologists have?

Our team consists of registered and licensed psychologists with advanced degrees in psychology and extensive experience. We maintain current knowledge of global professional standards and evidence-based practices.

Can I get psychological assessment services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive psychological assessments for various purposes including educational placement, career guidance, mental health diagnosis, and organizational evaluations.

What is your approach to therapy?

We use evidence-based, client-centered approaches tailored to individual needs. Our methods include cognitive-behavioral therapy, solution-focused therapy, and other proven psychological interventions that align with global best practices.
